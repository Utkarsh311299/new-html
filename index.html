Types of photography

There are many types of photography. Here are the list of its few types.

Potriat photography

One of the most common photography styles, portrait photography, or portraiture, aims to capture the personality and mood of an individual or group. Images may be candid or posed, full body or close-ups.

Nature photography

Nature photography is a wide range of photography taken outdoors and devoted to displaying natural elements such as landscapes, wildlife, plants, and close-ups of natural scenes and textures.

Street photography

A great street photograph is one that makes a person think or one that brings up a specific feeling, story, or idea. Street photography, a genre of photography that records everyday life in a public place.
